Please support our 2017 Building Campaign for the new Crossroads Women’s Center

Now more than ever, a space is needed where those whose survival and rights are under threat can come together across divisions to protect one another and build the movement for a more caring world.

The new Crossroads Women’s Center in Philadelphia has the potential to be a resource for such efforts, locally, nationally, and internationally.

We have pooled our resources for nineteen years and, with careful saving, were able to get a new building. We are now putting huge efforts into gathering pro bono professional advice and fundraising to develop it into a welcoming, accessible, environmentally and community friendly space.

But all the goodwill and volunteer effort will not pay for everything. We need your financial help for repairs and renovations estimated at over $300,000.

For 19 years the Crossroads Women’s Center has been a vital resource for the movement for a caring world. Please help us expand!

Building Crossroads is a project of Women in Dialogue, a 501c(3) registered nonprofit. See https://wccww.wordpress.com for more information.
Home to the Women's Fitness Club, Breastfeeding Initiative, Global Women's Strike, Every Mother is a Working Mother Network, DHS Give Us Back Our Children, Payday men's network and more, The Crossroads Women's Center is a grassroots, multi-racial, community-based, self-help center for advocacy, information and support for women all over the Philadelphia region on issues including poverty, welfare, child welfare, housing, criminal justice issues, domestic violence, sexual and racial violence, access to legal help, alternative health information and resources, discrimination, LGBTQ rights, disability resources, and more.
